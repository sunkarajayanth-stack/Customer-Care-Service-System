Advanced Customer Service Management System – FWD + DSA Project

This project combines front-end engineering and algorithmic thinking into a complete academic capstone. The web app models customer support operations while the DSA modules power sorting, searching, queue processing, assignment heuristics, and visualization.

  • Customer ticket lifecycle management.
  • Agent performance and assignment workflow.
  • Algorithm demos for educational use.
  • Local storage-backed persistence and simulation APIs.
